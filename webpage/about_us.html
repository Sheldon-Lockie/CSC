About Us

In 2012, Scott & Elizabeth Lockie, started Common Sense Compressor to fill the need for a caring, competent, and trustworthy compressed air and vacuum pump systems solution.

Leveraging over 30 years of industry knowledge and experience Common Sense Compressor is the preferred provider of new equipment, consultation, energy conservation, service, and service parts for many large and small industrial facilities in Idaho, Washington, and Montana.

Servicing some of the areas leading manufactures, timber mills and everyone in-between.

We represent companies such as our flagship business partner, Sullivan-Palatek, who share our belief that we will realize success only through integrity, honesty, and mutual respect of each other and our customers.
